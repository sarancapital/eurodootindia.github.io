About Us

A small, focused team working both sides of the journey

A narrow focus, a coordinated team, and a clear view of what we can and cannot promise you.

Why We Started EuroDoot

A founder's note

🖼️ Recommended: genuine founder photograph 700 × 700px · alt: "EuroDoot founder"

I have lived in Germany for more than fifteen years and have personally hired and relocated colleagues from India. I saw how much confusion exists around what is actually required, and how often candidates are told what they want to hear rather than what is true.

EuroDoot is narrow by design: one region, one sector, and one coordinated team, rather than a broad agency trying to cover every country and every industry.

Our Focus

Logistics, warehouse and selected Ausbildung in NRW

We do not work across every sector or every German state. Staying narrow lets our India and Germany teams actually know the roles, the employers and the realistic pathways, rather than spreading thin across unrelated industries.

Our Structure

One file, two teams

India candidate-support team

Reviews your profile, prepares documents, coordinates language planning and stays your main point of contact throughout.

Germany employer office

A locally registered company that deals directly with employers, recognition bodies and visa coordination on the ground in NRW.

Your file moves between these two teams as your case progresses. You are not transferred to an unrelated partner organisation partway through.

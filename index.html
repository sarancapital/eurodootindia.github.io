Why EuroDoot

One coordinated team, on both sides of your move

Most agents either sit in India and lose track once you land, or sit in Germany and never really know your background. We built EuroDoot to close that gap.

Our India team reviews your background, prepares your documents and keeps you updated in plain language. Our Germany office is a locally registered company that deals directly with employers, visa offices and the day-to-day reality of NRW.

The same file moves between both teams. You are not handed off to a stranger halfway through.

What We Actually Do

Four things that matter more than promises

We check eligibility first

Before anything else, we look honestly at whether your background fits a real visa pathway.

We focus narrowly

Logistics, warehouse and supply-chain roles in NRW. Not every sector, not every country.

We prepare you properly

CV, interview practice, document checks and a language plan suited to your target pathway.

We stay involved after arrival

Registration, banking and first-week orientation in NRW are part of the process, not an afterthought.

A Note From Our Founder

Why we started EuroDoot

I have lived and worked in Germany for over fifteen years, and I have personally gone through the process of bringing colleagues over from India: the paperwork, the waiting, the small mistakes that cost weeks.

EuroDoot exists because most of that process is unnecessarily confusing, and because a candidate's family deserves a straight answer about whether a move is realistic, not a sales pitch.

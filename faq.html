FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Grouped by topic. If your question isn't here, send it to us directly.

Eligibility

It depends on the role. A recognised degree, diploma or vocational certificate directly related to logistics, warehouse operations, or a technical trade usually counts. See the Eligibility page for detail.

Jobs and Ausbildung

Direct employment is a regular job matched to your existing qualification. Ausbildung is a multi-year training programme that ends in a recognised German qualification, with a lower training salary along the way. See Career Pathways.

Not as a standalone path to a German work visa. Most visa routes require a qualified role. We explain this in detail on the Eligibility page.

German Language

It depends on your pathway. Ausbildung generally targets B1; direct employment is assessed role by role. Full detail is on the Preparation page.
